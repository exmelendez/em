Prayer

We believe that prayer takes on many forms: every sigh is a prayer; our songs are prayer; every plié and lift is a prayer. We pray structured prayers in our community in worship on Sunday mornings and evenings. We pray as we help those in our community in need. We pray in our classrooms, when children and adults are learning. We encourage our members to pray always, to spend our lives in an attitude of prayer.
